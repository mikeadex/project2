Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does it take to generate a CV?

Answer: In most cases, you can have a fully customized CV ready within 10-15 minutes. Our process is designed to be quick, easy, and efficient, without compromising on quality.

2. Can i upload my existing CV to be rewritten?

Answer: Yes! You can upload your existing CV in formats like PDF or DOCX, and our AI will refine, format, and rewrite it to give it a fresh, professional look that aligns with industry standards.

3. Do you integrate with LinkedIn?

Answer: Absolutely! You can sync your LinkedIn profile with our platform, and we’ll transform the information into a well-structured, professional CV that’s ready to download or edit.

4. What kind of templates do you offer?

Answer: We offer a wide range of professionally designed templates tailored for different industries and career levels. You can choose from modern, creative, and classic designs, all optimized for both recruiters and Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

5. Can I customise my CV after its generated?

Answer: Yes, after your CV is generated, you can easily edit and tweak any section to better match the job you’re applying for. We give you full control over customization, allowing for as many revisions as needed.

6.Is my personal data safe on your platform?

Answer: We take data privacy very seriously. All your information is securely encrypted, and we comply with all major privacy regulations to ensure that your data is handled responsibly and kept confidential.

7. Do you offer support for specific industries?

Answer: Yes! Whether you're in tech, finance, marketing, or healthcare, our platform tailors your CV to your industry, highlighting the skills and experiences that matter most for the roles you're pursuing.
